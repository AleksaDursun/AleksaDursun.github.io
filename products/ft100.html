FLINK FT 100

FT100 Digital Home Programmable Thermostat

FT100 is a device that switches the heating system on and off as necessary. Sensing the air temperature it switches the heating on and off according to your pre-set schedule. The device is wire connected to your heating system.

  • Power supply: two AA batteries
  • Display accuracy: 0.5 째C
  • Contact capacity: 5A 230V
  • Working environment temperature 0-50 째C
  • Range of temperature adjustment: 5-35 째C
  • Range of temperature display: 0-40 째C
  • Output: switch relay
  • Running program: Set per 1 week as cycle
  • Instalation: wall or battery seat
  • Size: 86x86x29

Using a programmable thermostat, you can adjust the times you turn on the heating or air-conditioning according to a pre-set schedule. Programmable thermostats can store and repeat multiple daily settings. Even though thermostats can be adjusted manually, programmable thermostats will avoid any discomfort by returning temperatures to normal before you wake or return home.
